Metro Line Details

Aqua Line (Line 3)

Aqua Line 3 connects Aarey JVLR to Cuffe Parade and brings fast north-to-south metro travel through airport corridors, central Mumbai, and south Mumbai.

27 stations Mainly underground corridor Airport and city-core connectivity

Line Highlights

Route information

  • Runs from Aarey JVLR to Cuffe Parade
  • Connects airport terminals, Bandra Kurla Complex, Dadar, CSMT, and Churchgate
  • Offers strong interchange access with suburban rail and other metro corridors
