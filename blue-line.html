Blue Line (Line 1)

Blue Line 1 connects Versova to Ghatkopar and serves as a strong east-west metro corridor across Mumbai with important rail and metro interchanges.

12 stations Elevated alignment Opened on 8 June 2014

Line Highlights

Route information

  • Important interchanges at Andheri, Western Express Highway, Marol Naka, and Ghatkopar
