Metro Line Details

Green Line (Line 4)

Green Line 4 will connect Mira Road, Thane, Ghatkopar, and Wadala, while its branch corridor extends southward from Anik Nagar Bus Depot toward CSMT and SPM Circle.

38 main-line stations 15 branch-line stations Elevated and underground sections

Line Highlights

Route information

  • Main corridor runs from Shivaji Chowk (Mira Road) to Bhakti Park (Wadala)
  • Branch corridor extends from Anik Nagar Bus Depot to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus and SPM Circle
  • Improves east-west and suburb-to-city access through Thane, Ghatkopar, and Wadala
