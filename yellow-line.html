Metro Line Details

Yellow Line (Line 2A / 2B)

Yellow Line extends from Dahisar (East) to Mandale and strengthens suburb-to-city travel across both western and eastern corridors.

37 stations Elevated alignment 2A and 2B sections

Line Highlights

Route information

  • Connects Dahisar (East) to Mandale
  • Major interchanges include Dahisar (East), Andheri (West), Bandra, Income Tax Office, Eastern Express Highway, and Mankhurd
  • Combines the 2A and 2B sections into a longer cross-city metro corridor
