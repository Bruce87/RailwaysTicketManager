Metro Line Details

Red Line (Line 7 / 9)

Red Line combines the northern Line 9 extension with Line 7 to create a strong suburban-to-airport corridor from Mira-Bhayandar and Dahisar to Andheri East and Airport T2.

23 stations Elevated and underground sections Airport access corridor

Line Highlights

Route information

  • Runs from Subhash Chandra Bose Stadium to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport - T2
  • Links Mira-Bhayandar, Dahisar, Goregaon, Jogeshwari, Gundavali, and the airport corridor
  • Includes important interchanges with Yellow, Blue, Aqua, and future metro lines
